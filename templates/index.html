Prediction Skin Cancer

The dataset used to perform the training model prediction uses HAM 10000 dataset which contains 10000 images that classify 7 cell types (classes) of skin cancer.

Of the three types of malignant cancer, Melanoma (mel) is the most dangerous, Basal Cell Carcinoma (bcc) rarely spreads throughout the body but must be removed immediately and Actinic Keratoses (akiec) are slow growing and can often be treated without surgery.

Types of benign skin cancer (Benign) -> Melanocytic Nevi (nv), Benign Keratosis (bkl), Vascular Lesions (vasc), Dermatofibroma (df).

Types of malignant skin cancer (Malignant) -> Melanoma (mel), Basal Cell Carcinoma (bcc), Actinic Keratoses (akiec).

This application is only a screening medium for classifying skin cancer types, it is predictive so that the results are more certain, further examination must be carried out by a dermatologist.
